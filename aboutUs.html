If your favorite hobby sitting down in front of your monitor or televsion, picking up a controller, and getting lost in a world of someone else's creation, then you are just like us. We set out to create a site where you could rate games and get a list back in order from best to worst. We plan on expanding the catalog often, so if you don't see some of your games on the list, don't worry we are working on it.
